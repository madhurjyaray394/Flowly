---

## Why Flowly?

Most productivity apps are powerful but overwhelming.  
Flowly is built for people who just want to **start and make progress**.

- No databases to design
- No templates to configure
- No learning curve
- Just goals and tasks that make sense

---

## Who is Flowly for?

- Students
- Solo builders
- Creators
- Anyone who wants productivity **without complexity**

If Notion feels like too much, Flowly is for you.

---

## Core Ideas

- Goals drive tasks (not the other way around)
- Everything stays simple and connected
- Calm, focused UI
- Progress over perfection
